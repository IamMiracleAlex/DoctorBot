About

DoctorBot is a simple Bot that helps individuals and professionals to have extensive knowledge about a medical condition. To achieve this, it accepts keywords or phrases of these medical conditions.

For instance, you are sick or want to know about a medical condition, simply use keywords or phrase such as: stomach ache, pregnancy, headache, typhoid, malaria and typhoid, hiv, ebola, etc

Fun Fact

Having a conversation with DoctorBot is never boring... It understands basic human greetings e.g: Hello, Hi, Hey!, Hola! etc - with other forms of validation e.g: sure? alright, cool, etc. As well as: how are you?, how are you doing? etc. Make sure you exchange pleasantries with DoctorBot.
